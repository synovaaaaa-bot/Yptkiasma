## üìã Common Questions

### Q: Where do I start?
**A:** Read [DEPLOY-NOW.md](./DEPLOY-NOW.md) for the quickest path to deployment.

### Q: What was the Vercel error about?
**A:** The `figma:asset` import issue. It's fixed now! Details in [CHANGELOG.md](./CHANGELOG.md).

### Q: Do I need to replace logos before deploying?
**A:** No! You can deploy with placeholder logos now and replace them later. See [LOGO-REPLACEMENT-GUIDE.md](./LOGO-REPLACEMENT-GUIDE.md).

### Q: How long does deployment take?
**A:** Usually 2-3 minutes on Vercel after you push to GitHub.

### Q: Can I use a custom domain?
**A:** Yes! Instructions in [DEPLOYMENT.md](./DEPLOYMENT.md).

### Q: What if something breaks?
**A:** Check the Troubleshooting section in [DEPLOYMENT.md](./DEPLOYMENT.md).
